Online Test Instructions

Please read the following instructions carefully before starting your online test:

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection throughout the test.
  • Do not refresh or close the browser window during the test.
  • Each question is mandatory and must be answered before proceeding.
  • Time limit will be displayed; manage your time wisely.
  • Once submitted, you cannot retake the test.
  • If you face any technical issues, contact support immediately.
Good luck!
